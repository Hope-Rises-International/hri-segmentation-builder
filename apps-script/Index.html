Step 2: Configure Segment Groups

Toggle waterfall positions ON/OFF for this campaign. Changes affect who gets mailed. GROUP toggles (Cornerstone, Sustainer, New Donor, Major Gift, Mid-Level, Mid-Level Prospect) — OFF removes those donors from the universe. RFM toggles (Active Housefile, Lapsed, Deep Lapsed) — OFF skips the assignment gate; donors stay in the universe and may route to other ON positions.
